Why Study in the UK?

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

1-Year Master's Degrees

Unlike most countries, standard Master's programs in the UK take only one year to complete. This saves you significant tuition and living costs and gets you into the workforce faster.

2-Year Graduate Route Visa

International students who successfully complete an undergraduate or master's degree can stay in the UK for two years to work or look for work, gaining valuable international experience.

Free Healthcare

By paying the Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS) during your visa application, you gain access to the UK's National Health Service (NHS), providing free medical care during your studies.

What is the actual cost of studying in the UK from Hyderabad?

Tuition fees at UK universities typically range from £12,000 to £25,000 per year (approx. ₹13 Lakhs to ₹27 Lakhs). Living expenses are mandated by the UK government: £1,023 per month outside London (approx. ₹11 Lakhs/year) and £1,334 per month inside London (approx. ₹14.5 Lakhs/year).

What is the Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) and how does the visa work?

A CAS (Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies) is a virtual document issued by UK universities once you accept an offer and pay the initial deposit. You cannot apply for a UK Student Visa without a CAS. The Student Visa requires showing bank statements holding the tuition fees and living costs for a consecutive 28-day period.

Does the UK offer a Post-Study Work (PSW) visa?

Yes. The UK Graduate Route allows international students who complete their undergraduate or postgraduate degree to stay and work in the UK for 2 years (3 years for PhD graduates) with no minimum salary or sponsorship requirements.
