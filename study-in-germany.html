Why Study in Germany?

The Engineering and Innovation Hub of Europe

Zero or Low Tuition Fees

Most public universities in Germany charge no tuition fees for domestic and international students alike. You only pay a small semester contribution (usually €150-€300).

English Taught Programs

While learning German is helpful for living, hundreds of master's degree programs—especially in STEM fields—are taught entirely in English.

18-Month Job Seeker Visa

After graduating from a German university, you are entitled to an 18-month job seeker visa to find employment related to your field of study in Europe's largest economy.

Your Journey to Germany

A transparent, step-by-step process

1. APS Certification (Month 1-2)

The APS certificate is mandatory for Indian students applying to Germany. We guide you through the exact documentation required to get this certified without delays.

2. University Applications (Month 3-4)

We help you navigate the complex uni-assist portal and direct university applications, ensuring your SOPs and academic credits align with German standards.

3. Blocked Account & Visa (Month 5-6)

Once admitted, we assist in setting up your Coracle, Expatrio, or Fintiba Blocked Account, securing travel insurance, and filing your National Visa application.
