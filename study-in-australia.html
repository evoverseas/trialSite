What is the actual cost of studying in Australia from Hyderabad?

Tuition fees at Australian universities typically range from AUD 22,000 to AUD 45,000 per year (approx. ₹12 Lakhs to ₹25 Lakhs). The Australian Department of Home Affairs requires showing evidence of living costs equal to at least AUD 29,710 per year (approx. ₹16 Lakhs/year) to cover accommodation, food, and transport.

What is the Genuine Student (GS) requirement?

The Genuine Student (GS) requirement is a targeted screening system that replaces the old GTE format. It evaluates your background, career goals, link to India, and financial viability to confirm you are coming to Australia for a genuine study purpose. A strong GS statement is critical to preventing visa refusals.

What are the post-study work rights in Australia?

Under the Temporary Graduate Visa (Subclass 485), students who complete a registered degree in Australia can stay and work for 2 to 4 years depending on their qualification level (2 years for Bachelors/Masters by coursework, 3 years for Masters by research, and 4 years for PhDs).
