What is the actual cost of studying in the USA from Hyderabad?

The annual cost of studying in the USA consists of tuition fees and living expenses. State-funded public universities cost between ₹15 Lakhs and ₹25 Lakhs per year, while private universities range from ₹25 Lakhs to ₹45 Lakhs per year. Living expenses (rent, food, insurance) typically range from ₹8 Lakhs to ₹12 Lakhs per year, depending on the city.

How does the US student visa (F-1) application process work?

Once admitted, you receive an I-20 Form from the university. The steps are: 1) Pay the SEVIS fee ($350), 2) Complete and submit the DS-160 visa form, 3) Book Biometric and Consular interview appointments, and 4) Attend the physical visa interview. EV Overseas provides comprehensive mock visa interviews and financial documentation audits to guarantee high visa success.

Can I work in the USA after completing my degree?

Yes. Under the Optional Practical Training (OPT) program, international students can work in the US for 12 months after graduation. Students who complete a DHS-approved STEM degree qualify for a 24-month STEM OPT extension, allowing them to work in the USA for a total of 3 years (36 months).
